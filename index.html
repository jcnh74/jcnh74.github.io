All of your communications and contacts in one place – available privately and securely across all your devices – filtered and organized the way you want to see them.

Channel Navigation

Channels are a core feature of the Ekko user experience. Channels allow you to filter and organize message streams regardless of message type. Channels are navigated by swiping left and right to change channels and scrolling up and down to view individual messages and select one or more messages to expand.

Channel Tuner

Channels are created and managed using the Channel Tuner. Channels can be based on accounts, contacts and labels and arranged in any order. There are also default channels such as All Messages, All Posts, Trash, Sent, Archived and Drafts. Channels can be visible or hidden for custom views on the fly.

Touch & Hold Menu

Within an Ekko channel, there is no need to expand a message to reply, archive, delete, or label it. Simply touch and hold over any message in any channel and then roll over the appropriate icon to quickly manage your inbox and responses. Ekko’s touch and hold menu also lets you make any message a ToDo by creating custom tasks and setting reminders and due dates.

Message Composer

The Ekko message composer is one of Ekko’s most powerful features. It lets you send a single message as any combination of email, chat, SMS, Facebook and Twitter all at the same time. Your options are defined by the accounts you link to Ekko. In addition you can send any type of attachments including links, photos, images, videos, documents, files, audio and drawings.

ToDos

The ToDo section of the folder icon reveals rich ways to star, create custom tasks, reminders and due dates for a task. There is also a dedicated ToDo section of the app that enables you to view and manage tasks in one place.

Privacy + Security

Your privacy and security are top priorities at Ekko. Ekko does not scan your messages, profile you, use cookies or invade your privacy in any way. Your messages are secure, backed-up and available across all your devices under password protection and industry-standard encryption technologies. The Ekko message composer also allows you to apply message-specific self-destruction, password-protection, enhanced encryption and redaction.

Linking Accounts

There is an extensive and growing list of accounts types and contacts you can link to Ekko, including Gmail, Facebook, Twitter, Yahoo, iCloud, Outlook and AOL, as well as IMAP and POP email accounts. Ekko can also provide you with a secure Ekko email address as well as an Ekko phone number that supports text messaging and visual voicemail all within your Ekko channels.
