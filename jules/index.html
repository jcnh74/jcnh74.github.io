Organize. Save. Share.

Imagine being able to access all of your critical home and family information — appliance serial numbers, vehicle license plates, receipts for that kitchen remodel you did years ago — in an instant. Plus share select items with realtors, contractors, even neighbors.

Handy, right? That's Jules.

Powered by the HomeIQ Cloud platform and a friendly human touch, Jules digitizes your essential documents and possessions, and stores them securely. Making it a breeze to tap and manage from anywhere, 24/7.

Just as key, Jules saves you — and your entire home servicing ecosystem — time and money. Warranty notifications. Property value assessments. Comparative insurance analysis. All at your fingertips.

From organizing to sharing, Jules adds value. Peace of mind, too.
